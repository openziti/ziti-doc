<h3>In OpenZiti You Will Find</h3>

* A zero trust [overlay mesh network](../../reference/glossary.mdx#network-overlay-overlay) composed of a controller, edge routers and fabric routers
* [Numerous SDKs](../../reference/developer/sdk/index.mdx) making it easy to embed zero trust principles directly into your application
* [Tunneling applications](../../reference/tunnelers/index.mdx) providing zero trust access to those applications you cannot embed zero trust into directly
* Zero trust principles such as [authorize before connect](../../learn/core-concepts/security/authentication/auth.md),
[continual authorization](../../learn/core-concepts/security/authorization/auth.md),
[least-privilege access](../../learn/core-concepts/security/authorization/policies/overview.mdx)

OpenZiti makes it **easy** to embed zero trust, programmable networking directly into your app. With OpenZiti you can have **zero trust, high performance networking on any Internet connection**, without VPNs!
